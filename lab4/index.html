Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task- How to make chocolate chip cookies

    Heat oven to 375ºF.
    Stir 2 ¼ cups of flour with 1 tsp of baking soda and ½ tbsp salt; set aside.
    In a large mixing bowl, beat 1 cup of butter with ¾ cup of sugar, and ¾ cup of brown sugar at medium speed until creamy.
    Add 2 eggs and 2 tsps of vanilla, one at a time. Mix on low speed until incorporated.
    Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture. Stir in 2 cups of chocolate chips.
    Place a tablespoon of dough on cookie sheets.
    Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chip cookies are golden brown.
